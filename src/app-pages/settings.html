Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save a recipe?

▼

Click the "Save Recipe" button on the recipe details page to save it to your favorites.

Can I customize my meal plan?

▼

Yes, you can drag and drop meals into different days or remove them entirely. You can also edit meals by clicking on them.

How do I generate a shopping list?

▼

Once you've created your meal plan, click the "Generate Shopping List" button. The app will automatically create a list of all ingredients needed for your planned meals.

Can I filter recipes based on dietary restrictions?

▼

Yes! Use the diet filter dropdown menu in the recipe search to find recipes that match your dietary needs (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, etc.).

How do I contact support?

▼

Use the "Contact Us" form above to send us a message, and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.

Can I share my meal plan with others?

▼

Currently, meal plans are personal to your account. However, you can export your shopping list and share it with family members. You can download or email the shopping list.

How do I enable dark mode?

▼

Go to Settings and toggle the "Dark Mode".

How often are new recipes added?

▼

Our recipe database is updated weekly with new, seasonal recipes and user favorites.
